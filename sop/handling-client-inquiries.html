Handling Client Inquiries - Onboarding Responsibilities

This section of the document is extremely important for us to get acquainted and comfortable talking about with clients to ensure we are managing expectations correctly and staying within our scope, role, and responsibilities.

⚖️ The Key Distinction

Any documents produced by GNT are strictly between the Broker and the Client. These are required for regulatory compliance and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) laws.

  • Our Role: We facilitate the onboarding process.
  • Legal Boundary: Velantra is not a party to these agreements and has no control over their terms or structure.

🛠️ How to Handle Client Inquiries

We should remain the primary point of contact for onboarding. Avoid "looping" the client back to the sales team for general setup questions, as that creates friction.

  • General Questions (High Level):
    • If a client asks what a document like the BSA is for, provide a high-level explanation.
    • Note: I’ve added a brief "BSA/ICA Overview" to the FAQ section of our onboarding doc for your quick reference.
  • Technical/Legal Deep Dives:
    • If a client begins questioning specific clauses or the "nitty-gritty" of the legal language.
    • Action: Help the client compose a GNT Helpdesk Ticket detailing their specific questions. This ensures they get an official answer from the Broker’s legal team.
  • Requests for Sales Reps:
    • If they ask to speak to their original sales rep, acknowledge the request but offer immediate support first to keep the momentum.
    • "I can certainly reconnect you with [Name]. However, I’m here specifically to support your entire onboarding process—what questions can I help clear up for you right now?"
