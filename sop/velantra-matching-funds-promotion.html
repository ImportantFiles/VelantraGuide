Velantra Matching Funds Promotion SOP

Promotion Overview

  • Promotion Launch: Monday (insert exact date)
  • New clients can receive matching funds up to their license fee, helping them scale strategically without overexposure.
  • Duration: 90 days from account activation

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Full Match Eligible

  • License Fee: $15,000
  • Deposit: $15,000+
  • Matching Funds: $15,000
  • Client gets full license-value support for 90 days

Scenario 2: Partial Funding

  • License Fee: $15,000
  • Deposit: $5,000
  • Matching Funds: $5,000
  • Match is capped by actual funded capital

Scenario 3: Large Deposit

  • License Fee: $15,000
  • Deposit: $100,000
  • Matching Funds: $15,000
  • Match is capped by license fee

Think of this as “capital support on demand,” not a lump-sum bonus.

Scope

  • Account Managers – Client growth & monitoring
  • Closers / Sales Team – Relationship management & retention
  • Support (GNT Team) – Fund processing & tracking

Roles & Responsibilities

Account Managers

  • Monitor client performance and equity
  • Identify when additional capital is justified
  • Submit matching fund requests
  • Ensure proper tracking and responsible scaling

Closers / Sales Team

  • Conduct 90-day follow-ups
  • Request Trustpilot reviews
  • Request video testimonials
  • Evaluate eligibility for promotion extension

Support (GNT Team)

  • Review and process fund requests
  • Confirm approvals and allocations
  • Maintain accurate internal records

Process Workflow

Step 1: Client Onboarding

  • Confirm license payment
  • Verify initial deposit
  • Explain matching fund eligibility and 90-day timeframe

Step 2: Initial Fund Allocation

  • Matching funds are not fully deployed upfront
  • Allocation depends on trading activity, risk management, and performance

Step 3: Requesting Additional Matching Funds

  • Evaluate performance, equity, and drawdown
  • Submit request with full details

Step 4: Approval & Allocation

  • Support team reviews request
  • Funds are allocated once approved

Step 5: Ongoing Monitoring

  • Track drawdowns and exposure
  • Prevent overleveraging
  • Ensure disciplined scaling

Step 6: 90-Day Review

  • Reach out to client
  • Request review and testimonial
  • Evaluate performance and engagement

Step 7: Promotion Extension

  • Based on performance and engagement
  • Not guaranteed

Important Notes

  • Matching funds are a privilege, not an entitlement
  • Capital must be deployed responsibly
  • Communication must be clear and proactive
  • All actions must be documented

Compliance & Tracking

  • Track funds allocated
  • Track request dates
  • Maintain approval history
