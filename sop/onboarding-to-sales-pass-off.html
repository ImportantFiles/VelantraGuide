Onboarding to Sales Pass Off

In situations where you need to flag a situation to the sales team, or pass them back to sales, please follow the below SOP to ensure everything is being notified.

When you need to flag a situation for the sales team:

  • Leave Internal Comment in GHL (tag sales rep)
  • Drop the information in the velantra-onboarding-team channel in Slack
  • Tag sales rep, leadership, Q (admin), and Alex T

Reminder:

  • YOU are the person responsible for carrying through the resolution for this situation.
  • If you do not receive a response within 48hrs, then respond to your initial message and:
    • Retag the main sales rep and their Manager
    • Check the box, “Also send to _________”
