Maven

Maven is an automated Forex trading system designed to provide a systematic, hands-off approach to the currency markets. By utilizing the M15 (15-minute) timeframe, the system balances active market participation with a filtered approach that avoids unnecessary "noise" found in faster timeframes.

Core Trading Philosophy

  • Data-Driven Entries: The system identifies entry points using technical indicators to detect when a currency pair is overextended or ready for a move.
  • Precision Timing: By operating on 15-minute charts, Maven captures intraday trends and reversals, allowing for multiple trading opportunities throughout the day without the high frequency of a scalping bot.
  • Adaptive Behavior: The system uses specific "filters" to stay out of the market during periods of extreme uncertainty, such as when spreads are too high or when price spikes are too sudden.

Market Coverage

  • Current Focus: AUD/CAD, GBP/AUD, NZD/CAD, EUR/GBP, and GBP/CAD.
  • Strategic Evolution: The system is regularly refined; for example, it previously included EUR/SGD but has since been optimized to focus on the current pair set for better performance.

Risk Management & Safety

  • Volatility Protection: Maven includes a "Candle Size Filter," which prevents it from entering trades during chaotic market moves that could lead to unfavorable entries.
  • Drawdown Safeguards: A built-in "Max Drawdown Filter" acts as a circuit breaker.
  • Cost Control: The system monitors the "spread" and executes trades only when efficient.

How it Behaves

Maven is designed to be a "set and forget" assistant. It continuously monitors the charts 24/5, automatically opening, managing, and closing trades based on its internal rules. This removes the emotional stress of manual trading while ensuring that opportunities are never missed.
